Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Still Water Grace ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our content and services across social media platforms.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide: If you contact us, comment on our posts, or interact with our content, we may receive your username, comments, and messages as provided through the social media platform.

Automatically collected information: We may receive aggregated analytics data from social media platforms (such as post impressions, reach, and engagement metrics). This data is anonymized and does not personally identify you.

We do not collect: We do not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information unless you voluntarily provide it to us.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we receive to:

  • Improve our content and posting strategy
  • Respond to comments and messages
  • Analyze engagement trends (using aggregated, non-personal data)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

Our content is hosted and distributed through third-party platforms including:

  • Instagram (Meta Platforms, Inc.)
  • Facebook (Meta Platforms, Inc.)
  • TikTok (ByteDance Ltd.)

Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they collect and use your data. We encourage you to review their policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or the safety of others
  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform (under confidentiality agreements)

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information we hold. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our social media channels.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through our social media channels or at our website.
