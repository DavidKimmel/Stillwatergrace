1. ManyChat DM Automation

When a follower comments a trigger keyword on a post, ManyChat auto-sends them a DM with the devotional PDF download link and Kit landing page signup. This simultaneously boosts comment count (algorithm signal) and grows the email list.

Setup (Manual — Free Plan)

  1. Create a ManyChat account at manychat.com
  2. Connect to the @stillwatergrace Instagram account
  3. Create a Keyword Automation:
    • Trigger keyword: PEACE
    • Trigger type: Comment contains keyword
    • Apply to: All posts (or specific posts tagged in ManyChat)
  4. Build the DM flow:
    • Message 1: "Thanks for commenting! Here's your free 7-day devotional — Finding Peace in Every Season. Download it here: [R2 PDF URL]"
    • Message 2 (delay 30s): "Want daily encouragement in your inbox? Join our community: [Kit landing page URL]"
  5. Add the keyword CTA to 1-2 posts per week (faith_friday is the natural fit):
    "Comment PEACE and we'll send you our free 7-day devotional"
Free plan limits: 1,000 contacts, 1 keyword trigger. Sufficient for the 0→1,000 follower phase. Upgrade to Pro ($15/mo) when contact limit is reached.

Code Changes (Optional, Minimal)

No code changes are strictly required — ManyChat is configured entirely in their UI. Optionally, add a manychat_cta hint to the faith_friday.jinja2 prompt so Claude includes the keyword CTA in those captions specifically.

3. Daily Engagement Playbook

This is the manual engagement routine. The algorithm rewards accounts that actively participate in the community, not just post content.

Daily Routine (15-20 minutes)

ActionTimeDetails
Reply to comments5 minReply to every comment on your posts within 1 hour of posting. Ask a follow-up question to keep the thread going.
Engage with niche accounts10 minVisit 10 accounts in the faith niche. Leave genuine 2-3 sentence comments on their posts (reference specific content, not "nice post"). Search hashtags: #christianmom, #faithandfamily, #bibleverseoftheday, #marriagegoals, #christianparenting
Like niche content2 minLike 20-30 posts from accounts in your niche hashtag feeds
Respond to DMs3 minReply to all DMs — even "thanks" messages. Personal touch builds community.

Weekly Routine (30 minutes)

ActionDetails
Follow niche accountsFollow 20-30 accounts that actively engage (not ghost followers). Look for accounts that comment on competitors' posts.
Review Instagram InsightsWhich posts got highest reach? Which reels got shares? Double down on those content types and tones next week.
Clean follow listUnfollow non-engagers after 7 days. Keep follow/following ratio below 1.5:1.
Review ManyChat statsHow many keyword comments? DMs sent? Conversion to Kit signups?

Engagement Quality Rules

  • Never: "Nice post!", "Amen!", one-word comments on other accounts
  • Always: Reference something specific — "This verse hit different today. Praying for everyone going through that season of waiting."
  • Goal: Your comment should make the poster AND their followers want to click your profile
